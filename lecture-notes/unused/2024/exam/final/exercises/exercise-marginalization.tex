\subsection{Marginalization}
\totalscore{3}

For a simple SCM $M$ with endogenous variables $V$, and any nonempty subset $L \subset V$, we have $G(M_{\setminus L}) \subseteq G(M)^{\setminus L}$. 
Construct a simple SCM and a nonempty subset $L \subset V$ for which $G(M_{\setminus L}) \neq G(M)^{\setminus L}$ and explain why the two graphs are not equal.
\points{There are many possible solutions. 2 points for a correct solution, 1 point for the explanation.}
